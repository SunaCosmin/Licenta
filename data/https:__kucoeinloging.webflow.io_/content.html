KuCoin Login Process

  1. Visit the KuCoin Website: Open your preferred web browser and navigate to the KuCoin website. The website address is https://www.kucoin.com.
  2. Locate the "Login" Button: On the KuCoin homepage, you'll find the "Login" button in the top right corner. Click on it to initiate the login process.
  3. Enter Your Credentials: To log in to your KuCoin account, you'll need to provide your registered email address or username and your password.
  4. Complete the CAPTCHA: KuCoin employs security measures to protect user accounts. You may be required to complete a CAPTCHA to verify that you're not a bot.
  5. Two-Factor Authentication (2FA): If you've enabled two-factor authentication for added security, you'll need to provide the necessary authentication code. This code is usually generated by a 2FA app like Google Authenticator or sent to your registered email.
  6. Click "Log In": Once you've entered your credentials and completed any necessary security steps, click the "Log In" button to access your KuCoin account.
