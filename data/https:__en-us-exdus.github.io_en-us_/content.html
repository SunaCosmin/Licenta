Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Exodus Wallet and cryptocurrency management.

What is Exodus Wallet?

Exodus is a multi-cryptocurrency wallet that allows users to store, manage, and exchange various digital assets in one secure application. It features an intuitive interface, built-in exchange functionality, and robust security measures.

Is Exodus Wallet secure?

Yes, Exodus implements industry-standard security measures. Your private keys never leave your device, and the wallet uses advanced encryption to protect your data. Additionally, you can secure your wallet with a password and recovery phrase.

How many cryptocurrencies does Exodus support?

Exodus Wallet supports over 150 cryptocurrencies and tokens, including Bitcoin, Ethereum, Solana, Cardano, and many more. The list of supported assets is continuously expanding with regular updates.

Can I exchange cryptocurrencies within Exodus?

Yes, Exodus features a built-in exchange that allows you to swap between supported cryptocurrencies directly within the wallet interface, without having to use external exchanges.
