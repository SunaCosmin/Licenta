How to find and install the MetaMask app or extension?

MetaMask allows traders to install the wallet app for mobile and extension for PCs. If you are not familiar with the way to find and install the wallet, follow these steps elaborated below:

‍For Mobile users:
‍
1.Go to the application store
2.Find the app from the store
3.Tap the ‘Install’ or ‘Get’ option to install the app
4.Now, the MetaMask app will be added to your device

‍For PC users:

1.Open a browser, visit the www.Metamask.io/download site
2.From here, you need to get to the extension files
3.Choose your browser to proceed to the next page
4.Click the ‘Add to my browser’ to add a wallet

Simple steps to set up a new MetaMask Wallet

To create your MetaMask login account, all you need to do is, stay connected with the internet and then follow the steps that are explained below:
‍
1.Based on the device type, open a mobile app or browser extension
2.Now, reach to the ‘Create Wallet’ button and click on it
3.Once you are asked, get to the password fields to choose a password
4.Now, click ‘Next’ and then find the seed phrase details
5.Once asked, confirm the seed phrase in the respective field
6.Finally, find and click the ‘Finish’ button to complete the wallet setup process

How to access the MetaMask login account?

Accessing the MetaMask is quite easy and you can also login to your MetaMask account with the help of the password details:
‍
1.Get to the MetaMask app or browser extension and open them
2.When prompted, type the password details in the respective field
3.If the password is entered correctly, get to the ‘Unlock’ button and click it
4.Finally, you will be able to access the MetaMask dashboard

Method to recover MetaMask login account

Recovering a MetaMask account is quick and you can also complete this process if you have the MetaMask recovery seed phrase details. Now, you need to approach these steps that are given below to recover your wallet:

1Open the MetaMask wallet application or use its browser extension to proceed
2.Now, you need to select the ‘Use Secret Recovery Phrase’ option
3.Fill up the seed phrase and then type a new password for your account
4.Finally, get to the ‘Recover’ button to import your Metamask Login
