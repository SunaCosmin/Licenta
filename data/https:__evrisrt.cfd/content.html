Send a parcel

Send, track and return parcels

At Evri, we provide cheap and convenient parcel delivery services, using our network of over 18,000 friendly parcel Couriers.

We deliver over 700 million packages a year to over 220 countries and territories worldwide.

Use our Courier service online to send, track and return your parcels quickly and easily - all in one place. Just choose from our ParcelShops, Lockers or book a Courier Collection.

You can track parcels every step of the way using our website or App, and full tracking is included as standard with every parcel.

Why Evri?

Complete flexibility

Send or return your parcels with our network of parcel Couriers, using one of 10,000+ ParcelShops and Lockers located across the UK. Or, book door-to-door Couriers online for collection throughout the country.

A premium service

Whether you’re sending or returning a parcel with Evri, you’ll get full tracking and cover for up to £20, free of charge. Plus, you’ll get up to 3 parcel delivery attempts and the option to divert your parcel if needed.

Cheap courier service

Whether you’re using Standard or Next Day delivery, send or return a parcel using our handy Courier service, for a low price.

Sending lots of parcels is much FASTER

Sending lots of parcels? Sort them in seconds by connecting your Evri account with eBay, Amazon, Shopify, and more. You can also upload a CSV file - whatever's easiest! Our bulk integrations tool will save you time and hassle on every parcel.

Tracked Courier service

When using Evri’s Courier service to send or return a parcel, you’ll get full tracking included in the cost of postage.

On the day of your parcel delivery, you’ll get a 1-hour delivery slot. This means you can plan your day around us.

If you’re not going to be in, don’t worry! Our tracking tool lets you divert your parcel or select a ‘Safe Place’ for our parcel Couriers to leave your items
