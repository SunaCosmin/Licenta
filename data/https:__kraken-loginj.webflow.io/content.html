How To Login A Kraken Account

1. Visit kraken.com.
2. Click on “Get Started.” or “Create Account” on the top right-hand corner of your screen.
3. You’ll be redirected to the “Create your account" page, where you have to enter your email address, username, and password. Note: you can’t change the username.
4. Check the agree box after reading the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. Then click the “Sign Up” button.
5. You'll receive an activation email in your inbox, which contains an activation key.
6. Activate your account by clicking the link in the email.
7. Confirm the password, finish the captcha if it comes up.
8. Click the “Activate Account” button.

How To Buy Bitcoin On Kraken

1. Sign in to your verified Kraken account
2. Deposit funds into your account.
3. Return to your trading dashboard and click the “Buy” button located at the “Buy Cryptocurrency” section.
4. Insert the amount of Bitcoin you want to purchase
5. Confirm the order
6. Click on “Submit Order.”

How To Send Bitcoin To Kraken Futures

1. Sign in to your Kraken account
2. Search for the “Funding” tab
3. Click the “Transfer” button for the cryptocurrency you want to transfer to Kraken futures. In this case, Bitcoin.
4. Enter the amount you want to send.
5. Click the “Review Transfer” button. After, you click on the “Confirm Transfer” button.
6. You'll get a notification at the bottom of the page confirming the transfer's success.
