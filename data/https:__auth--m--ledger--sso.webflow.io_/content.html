Step-by-Step Setup Guide for Ledger.com/Start

The detailed setup guide at Ledger.com/start will walk you through the steps required to secure your Ledger hardware wallet. Starting with unboxing, you'll learn how to connect your device to your computer or mobile phone using the included USB or Bluetooth cable. The guide will then help you download and install Ledger Live, the companion software for managing your crypto assets.

Next, you'll be guided through initializing your device, including choosing a secure PIN and writing down your recovery phrase. Ledger.com/start emphasizes the importance of keeping this phrase safe and offline, as it's the only way to recover your funds if you lose access to your device. Finally, you will learn how to add and manage different cryptocurrencies using Ledger Live..
