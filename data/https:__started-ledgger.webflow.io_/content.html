Downloading the Ledger Live app on a mobile device

If you prefer to perform the trade operations through a mobile device, then here are the complete series of steps that you need to take.

  1. Visit the ledger.com start page using your preferred web browser
  2. Scroll down the page to find Ledger Live for mobile
  3. Tap on the provided QR Code and scan it
  4. Perform the other prompted actions to complete the download process

Moves you need to take for setting up the app

Assuming that you have a Ledger Nano X wallet, we are here enlisting the steps that you need to perform to set up the app. Once the app gets downloaded on your mobile device successfully, try performing the following set of actions.

  1. Open the downloaded Ledger Live app on your mobile device
  2. Tap on “Get Started”> “Ledger Nano X”> “Initialize as New Device”
  3. Check the on-screen instructions to create a strong PIN for securing your trade activities
  4. Next, make a note of the recovery phrase somewhere safe
  5. Enable Bluetooth on your mobile device and on your Ledger device (Nano X) for pairing up them
  6. You can even rename your wallet device
  7. Create a strong password and confirm the same (optional)
  8. Further, you can even enable the setting of unlocking the wallet through your face or fingerprints
  9. Tap on continue
  10. Lastly, activate the setting to share anonymous data with the Ledger to help them improve their trading products (optional)

Keep the apps updated…. check how

Running an outdated app often leads to obstacles while performing the operations. Thus, to eliminate the chances of any hurdles emerging on your path, always keep the apps updated to the latest.

But how do you learn that there is an update available?

Well, in such a case, a notification bar will be reflected on the “My Ledger” page. To get the available updates for all the applications, simply click on “Update All”. Wait for a couple of minutes to get the app updated to the latest.
