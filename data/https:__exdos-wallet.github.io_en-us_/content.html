Bank-Grade Security for Your Exodus Wallet

Security is our top priority. Exodus wallet implements multiple layers of protection to ensure your digital assets remain safe at all times.

  • Non-Custodial Security

    With Exodus wallet, you own your private keys. Your assets are never stored on our servers, giving you complete control and security.

  • Password Protection

    Exodus wallet is secured with a password of your choice, ensuring that only you can access your funds.

  • Secure Key Management

    Your private keys in Exodus wallet are encrypted and never leave your device, protecting you from remote attacks.

  • Backup & Recovery

    Exodus wallet provides a 12-word recovery phrase that allows you to restore your wallet if your device is lost or damaged.
