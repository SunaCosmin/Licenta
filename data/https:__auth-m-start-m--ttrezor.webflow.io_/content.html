Trezor.io/Start® - Starting Up Your Device | Trezor®

To set up your Trezor device, visit trezor.io/start. Follow these steps:

  1. Connect your Trezor device to your computer using the USB cable.
  2. Download and install the Trezor Bridge to facilitate communication between your device and the computer.
  3. Access the Trezor Wallet through your browser.
  4. Follow the on-screen instructions to initialize your Trezor device.
  5. Create a new wallet and secure your recovery seed.
  6. Set up a PIN to protect your device.
  7. Once the setup is complete, you can start managing your cryptocurrencies securely.
