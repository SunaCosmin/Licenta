Steps to register yourself on the KuCoin Platform:

  1. First of all, launch your web browser and visit the official web page of kucoin.com.
  2. Once the web page is fully loaded or opened up, hit on the “Sign Up” button, located next to the “Log in” button.
  3. Now, you can either choose your phone number or email address to start the sign-up process. We are selecting the “Phone number” option as an example for completing our further steps.
  4. After clicking on the “Phone Number” tab, enter your phone number in the first text field.
  5. Create a strong and unique password for your KuCoin login and enter it in the second text field.
  6. After entering the password, hit on the “Sign Up” button.
  7. Enter the 6-digit verification code in the required text field that you have received via SMS from KuCoin and then simply hit on the “Activate Account”.
  8. In the final step, simply click on the “Verify” button to finish the entire process.

Steps for KuCoin login:

Follow these simple steps for your KuCoin login on its official website,

  1. Open your browser and visit the login page of the KuCoin platform.
  2. Now, click on the “Phone Number” or “Email” tab, which you have registered to your KuCoin account. And if you are already logged in to your KuCoin mobile App, then you can directly perform KuCoin login on the web by scanning the QR code.
  3. Type your registered email ID or phone number in the first text field.
  4. Enter your correct login password in the second text field and click on the “Log In” button.
  5. In the last step, you just have to complete the SMS verification process and then hit on the “Submit” button to finish the entire process of KuCoin login.
