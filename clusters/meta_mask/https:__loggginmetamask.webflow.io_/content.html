Downloading and setting up the wallet from scratch

To set up your wallet from scratch, you need to make sure that you have installed the wallet files from the official website only. Here are the steps to be followed:

  1. Begin the process by visiting https://metamask.io website
  2. Further, you can choose the "Download" option and choose your preferred file type
  3. After installing the wallet file, the wallet interface would open automatically
  4. Once you are there on the page, you can surely select the "Get Started" option
  5. When on the "Get Started" page, you need to select the option to set up your wallet
  6. Thereafter, you can read and agree to the given points
  7. Further, you can set up your MetaMask login password
  8. Next up, you can also take necessary actions to store and backup your Secret Recovery Phrase

Upon completing the whole process to set up your wallet, you need to sign in again to add an additional account. While you set up your wallet initially, you will be provided with a primary account without having to do anything. Now, let's take a dig into the process to sign into your wallet account.

A glance at the MetaMask login process

Signing into a MetaMask account is pretty easy because it just takes a few easy steps that you need to follow. Here are the steps:

  1. Begin the process by opening the downloaded MetaMask file
  2. On the main page, you'll see a blank space to enter your password
  3. In this space, just feed in the correct MetaMask login password
  4. Under it, find and select the "Unlock" option
  5. That's it!!! You'll be logged into your wallet account
