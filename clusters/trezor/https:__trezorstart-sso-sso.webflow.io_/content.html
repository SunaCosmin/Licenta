Trezor.io/start is the official starting point for setting up your Trezor hardware wallet and accessing the Trezor Suite app, a comprehensive tool for managing your cryptocurrencies. This platform guides users through the initial setup process, ensuring a secure and user-friendly experience. Trezor Suite offers a range of features, including portfolio tracking, buying and selling cryptocurrencies, and enhanced privacy options like Tor support. The app works seamlessly with Trezor devices, providing a secure interface for managing multiple cryptocurrencies while keeping private keys offline.
